Appendix 1: GEOS–Chem Tracers, by Simulation

In this Appendix, we shall enumerate the different types of GEOS–Chem simulations that you can perform. For each simulation, we shall provide a list of advected tracers.

A1.1 NOx–Ox–hydrocarbon–aerosol simulations

The tracers listed below are in general either long-lived (and must be transported in the model) or are "families" consisting of two or more molecules. For a complete list of all of the chemical species used in the GEOS–Chem chemistry mechanisms, please consult Appendix 6.

Also, tracers in BOLD ITALICS are carried as atoms C instead of molecules tracer. We assign to these a molecular weight of 12 g/mole. The number of moles C per moles tracer is listed below.

There are different chemistry mechanism options for the NOx–Ox–hydrocarbon–aerosol simulations. In the following table we give the tracers for each option: the standard mechanism, the SOA mechanism, the dicarbonyls mechanism and the Caltech isoprene mechanism.
