10. GEOS-Chem Species

The GEOS-Chem species database provides a central location for storing the physical properties of GEOS-Chem species, including names, indices, molecular weights, and Henry's law constants. It also stores logical flags that denote whether each species is advected, is dry deposited, or is wet scavenged.

all species are now just stored in the State_Chm%Species array.
